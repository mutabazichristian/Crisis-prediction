Required CSV Format

Your CSV file must include the following columns:

  • country: Country name
  • year: Year of observation (numeric)
  • exch_usd: Exchange rate to USD (numeric)
  • gdp_weighted_default: GDP-weighted default rate (numeric)
  • inflation_annual_cpi: Annual inflation rate (numeric)
  • systemic_crisis: Binary indicator (0 or 1)
  • banking_crisis: Target variable ("crisis" or "no_crisis")
