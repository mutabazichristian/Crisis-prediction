How to Use This Predictor

  • Country: Select the African country you want to analyze.
  • Year: Enter the year for which you want to make a prediction.
  • Exchange Rate (USD): The country's currency exchange rate to USD.
  • GDP Weighted Default: Default rate weighted by GDP.
  • Annual Inflation (CPI): Consumer Price Index annual inflation rate.
  • Systemic Crisis: Whether there's an existing systemic crisis (0=No, 1=Yes).

The model provides:

  • Crisis Prediction: Binary outcome (crisis or no crisis)
  • Probability: Likelihood of a banking crisis occurring
  • Risk Level:
    • Low Risk: Probability < 0.3
    • Medium Risk: Probability 0.3-0.7
    • High Risk: Probability > 0.7
